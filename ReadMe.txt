Game: Tic-Tac-Toe

Description:
Tic-Tac-Toe is a classic two-player game where players take turns marking spaces in a 3x3 grid with their respective symbols. 
The goal is to be the first to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of three of their symbols.


Game: Space Shooter

Description:
Space Invaders is a legendary arcade game. It's one of the earliest shooting games and remains an iconic title in video game history. 
In Space Invaders, players control a spaceship at the bottom of the screen, tasked with defending Earth from waves of descending alien invaders.


Game: Drag and Drop Dream House Builder

Description:
the Drag and Drop Dream House Builder, a game where you can design and construct your ultimate dream home with just a few clicks. This game offers an interactive and intuitive experience, allowing players to unleash their imagination and architectural prowess to craft the perfect home.
